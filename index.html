I am a strategic communications and media professional with over a decade of experience advancing labor, racial justice, and public interest campaigns. My work spans successful local, statewide, and regional political initiatives, including minimum wage campaigns, hospital tax efforts, and helping to develop a new healthcare plan serving over 10,000 working families within a monopolized insurance market.

With deep experience navigating the complex political landscapes of both New York City and the San Francisco Bay Area, I specialize in earned media strategies that elevate frontline voices and bring visibility to racial and economic justice issues, shaping public narratives and influencing policy outcomes.
